# Sabalist — Screenshot Captions (App Store + Google Play)

Paste-ready caption copy to overlay on your store screenshots. Captions are the
bold text you add on top of each screenshot image (not a field in the console).
Keep each headline short so it's readable as a thumbnail — App Store shows the
first 3 screenshots in search, and ~90% of users never scroll past them, so put
your strongest message first.

**Specs**
- App Store: up to 10 screenshots per device. Required sizes: 6.7" (1290×2796) and 6.5" (1242×2688); 12.9" iPad if you ship iPad.
- Google Play: 2–8 phone screenshots (min 320px, max 3840px, 16:9 or 9:16). Feature graphic required: 1024×500.
- Apple indexes screenshot caption text (since 2025), so naturally include keywords (buy, sell, marketplace, jobs, cars, rentals).

## Localization
Translate captions for priority markets before generating localized screenshot
sets. Start with **French** (West/Central Africa), then Arabic and Portuguese.
Keep the brand name "Sabalist" untranslated.

## Notes
- Avoid the words "best", "#1", or "free" inside the Google Play **title** (policy), but they are fine inside screenshot captions and the description.
- Show real, locale-appropriate listings (local currency, local goods) — generic stock content converts worse and can look untrustworthy.
