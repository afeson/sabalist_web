How to Request Account Deletion

1
Send an Email
Send an email from the email address associated with your Sabalist account to our support team.
2
Use the Correct Subject Line
In the subject line, write: "Delete My Account"
3
Include Your Account Information
In the email body, include:
  • Your account email address
  • Your phone number (if you used phone authentication)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
4

You will receive a confirmation email within 1-2 business days acknowledging your request.
5
Account Deletion Complete
Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days of your request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account profile (name, email, phone number)
  • All your active and inactive listings
  • Photos and images you uploaded
  • Saved favorites and preferences
  • Account activity history
  • Authentication credentials
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

Data Retention

For legal, security, and business integrity purposes, we may retain certain information even after account deletion:

  • Transaction records: Required for tax and legal compliance (anonymized)
  • Communication logs: Support tickets and emails (retained for 90 days)
  • Security logs: IP addresses and authentication logs (retained for fraud prevention)
  • Legal holds: Data subject to legal proceedings or investigations

All retained data is anonymized where possible and is not used for marketing or profiling purposes.

Deletion Timeframe

Your account will be fully deleted within 7 business days of receiving your deletion request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
