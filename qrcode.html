Sabalist - Expo QR Code

Scan with Expo Go on Android

URL: exp://10.0.0.192:8087

How to Test on Android:

  1. Make sure your Android device is on the same Wi-Fi network (10.0.0.x)
  2. Open the Expo Go app on your Android device
  3. Tap "Scan QR code"
  4. Point your camera at the QR code above
  5. The app will load with Firebase v9
  6. You should see the phone authentication form

Note: Web and iOS will show "Platform Not Supported" messages as configured.
